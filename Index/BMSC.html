BlueMinds Supply Chain Solution formerly known as BlueMinds Enterprises was incorporated in 2020 with an aim to make it easier for transporters to book a load and enable Clients to have access to the right truck at the right time for the right price.

At BlueMinds, we believe, our customers are the most important part of our business. Our unique approach to solve any complaint directly by our top management saves lots of time and has enabled us to get continuous business from many top brands like Sharpmint Ltd, Ample Foods, Amar Agro, Ariba Foods, DSNA Agro and many more.

We majorly deal in below verticals:

  1. Full Truck Load
  2. Part Truck Load
  3. Cold Storage
  4. Cross-docking of vehicles
